Equiple Guide

    Equiple - item guessing game based off Wordle. You have to guess equipment from the game Realm of the Mad God in 10 tries. Hints for you are six item attributes, which are:

  • Tier (Item Tier) - either "UT" (Untiered), "ST" (Set Tiered) or "Tiered". Reskinned items marked as "Reskin" afterwards, which makes 6 tiers in total. If target item is reskin, you will have to guess the reskin. For example: Doom Bow, Sun's Judgement and Bow of the Morning star.
  • Type (Slot Type) - self-explainatory, slot that item takes. Either "Weapon", "Ability", "Armor" or "Ring".
  • Shots (Number of Shots) - number of shots item fires. Yes, some items don't have any ;)
  • Range - range of the item. Items that technically have range, but as "max distance" or "radius" will have that value here instead. For example: traps, prisms, etc.
  • Xp Bonus (Fame Bonus) - xp bonus given by the item.
  • Feedpower - feedpower of the item.
    Items that have some type of mathematical modifiers will have either minimal value or zero, depending on the case.

There are two gamemodes: Daily and Normal.
    In Daily gamemode everyone is given the same item, and you can only do it once per day.
    In Normal you can play as much as you want and you can exclude reskinned items from the game.
Consecutive wins in both gamemodes increase your respective streak, losing on the other hand voids it. Best players show up in the Leaderboard.
  • You have 10 tries to guess the item.
  • You need to guess exact item that was guessed. You can end up in the situation where several items fit your guess.
  • Don't cheat!
